Cyber Class Dimsa Goes To UNS

  • by:Hafizh izzat & Lutfi Sakhi
  • date:November 22, 2024

Friday, 22 November 2024, SMP Darul Ihsan Muhammadiyah Sragen carried out a Comparative Study to the PTIK FKIP UNS Surakarta Study Program with all students of class 9 (B and E) Cyber ​​Class. The PTK UNS campus is at Campus V Pabelan Surakarta.

This activity was attended by 45 students, 4 accompanying teachers, lecturers and study program heads (Kaprodi) as well as dozens of PTIK UNS students.

I think you should also see the news about DIMSA FANTASTIC SHOW.

The activity was opened by Mr. Cucuk Wawan Budiyanto S.T., Ph.D. , as the Head of PTIK UNS Study Program enthusiastically welcomed the students, as well as the brothers and sisters of HMP MIKROPTIK UNS. After opening, students were explained about the profile of the PTIK study program, learned robotics and design, went around the campus/campus tour.
