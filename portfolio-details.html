Darul Ihsan Muhammadiyah Boarding School

Darul Ihsan Muhammadiyah Sragen is an Islamic educational institution located in Sragen Regency, Central Java. This Islamic boarding school is part of the Muhammadiyah organization which aims to produce young people who have noble character, knowledge, and play an active role in society, and have a good and strong understanding of religion. As part of Muhammadiyah, this Islamic boarding school has a vision to create a generation that has integrity, intelligence and good character.
